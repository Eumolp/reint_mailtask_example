- **05.04.2016** -> realurl

  - with realurl 2.0.0 to 2.0.10 the speaking urls aren't generated when task is executed
  - workaround: use realurl in version 2.0.11 and higher or version 1.13.6 and lower


Tested the extension with TYPO3 6.2.19 and 7.6.4.
